Projects

Ha'Garta

Boardgame

Ha'Garta is a game where players incarnate War Generals, leading their people into the reconquest on the mytical city. But there's not just the players who want to reconquer the city, there is also the first owner of the city. Players will have to make alliances to survive but there will be only one winner.

Bayou

Video Game

Bayou is a video game available on PC. Player play as Shayne, who's trapped in the Bayou, the swamp between the world of living and the world of death in voodoo. The player will have to find the exit of this swap, and for this, he will have to fight creatures and find his own way out.

Never Ending Disco Love

Video game

Never Ending Disco Love is a top-down car game where you need to drive around the city, catching dancers to have fun in your van. But be careful, police cars aren't far away and they seem to not like you.

Un train d'enfer

Video Game

This is a cooperation multiplayer game. The action takes place in a future where only train still in activity and connect fortified city with each other. Players are crew members of an armored train and they will have to communicate and cooperate together if they want to reach the next station.

Sailorz

Video Game

This is an experimental Game Design project. Players will have to control a pirate ship, only by going on the left and right side of the ship in ordor to dodge icebergs and collect treasures. We tryed to re-think the principle of a controller and how we can create fun with a new and creative installation.
