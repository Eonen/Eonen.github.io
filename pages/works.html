Ha'Garta

Boardgame

Ha'Garta is a game where players incarnates a War Generals, leading he's people into the reconquest on the mytical city. But there's not only player who want to reconquest the city, there is also the first owner the city. Players will have to make alliance to survive but there will be only one winner.

Bayou

Video Game

Bayou is a video game available on PC. Player incarnate Shayne, who's trapped in the Bayou, the swamp between the world of living and the world of death in voodoo. The player will have to find the exit of this swap, and for this, he will have to fight creatures and find he's own way out.
