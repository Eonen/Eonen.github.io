Technical Feature

This project in entirely made with Game Maker 1. The game use one pc as the principal server and all the other pc connected on the same network can join the game.

Best Features

The players are stuck in a train and they will have to make it work properly. The players will have to communicate via the game but not everbody can communicate with each other, their is a special room for that, one player will have to coordonate all the others players

Context

This is a school work with the subject "Cooperation game" in team of six. We made it in 1 month. We decided that our main feature will be the communication between players.

My role

In this project I made decision like core mecanics, univers and goals in the game. I then manage the team to coordonate The level designer and the programmers. I also did some code to help programmers and some level design too.

About the game

Un Train d'Enfer is a game where player will have to communicate well to dodge the obstacle on the railway or to kill the looters trying to destroy your train. The goal is simple, survive and go to the end on the railway. Sadly, your supply aren't infinite, you will have to stop sometimes on stations to get back some supply and also repair the damage taken.
